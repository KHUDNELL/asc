Our Process

The initial process involves determining exactly what type and how much damage you sustained. Then we review the type of roof you have to formulate a plan to move forward if need be. If this involves an insurance claim, we advise on how and when to file, and what works best in speaking with the insurance claims adjuster. All of these factors make a major difference in your success of getting the carrier to 'honor' his or her side of the policy. Remember, insurance carriers are not in the business to pay claims, but to collect premiums. And that is why you need an advocate such as Sewell Consulting.

If a claim is initiated, we meet with the adjuster or carrier representatives. We show them why they need to pay based on the damage, building codes, wind codes or condition of the roof. We speak their language, because we are in this business and know how to represent on equal footing.

Once the claim paperwork comes back, we go through it to verify that you have been compensated correctly. If not, we contact the carrier and submit the corrections (we utilize the same software they do to make certain your compensation is accurate). This ensures you that there will not be additional charges down the road.

Then we provide you with a list of certified, vetted, insured roofing companies that specialize in the type of repairs you are in need of. Not all companies are suited for all jobs. The companies recommended by us are ones we've used and are very satisfied with, time-and-time again.

During the roofing work, we verify you are getting above average products and quality workmanship. Then we help you file the required paperwork to finalize your claim and supplement for any additional items that were not apparent before construction began.

Finally, we verify or negotiate the warranties you will be getting, either through the manufacturer or the installer.
