Our Mission

Our number one job at Sewell Consulting is to be an advocate for the owner before, during and after roof damage. This comes with a tried and true, well-tested multifaceted approach that we implement on your behalf.

A simple analogy is as follows: If you total your Chevrolet and have replacement coverage, the insurance carrier wants to pay for a new Chevette. At Sewell Consulting, we realize you deserve top of the line, and we negotiate to have them pay for a new Corvette. Without a knowledgeable advocate, you have to take them at their word. Don't put all your trust (and money) in the hands of the insurance carrier. Call me today, and we'll work together to generate the best outcome for your situation.
